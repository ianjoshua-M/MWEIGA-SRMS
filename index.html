About The Mweiga SRMS

The Mweiga High School Student Results Management System is a web-based application designed to allow school admins to log in and add students to the database, teachers to log in and input individual raw subject results for each student or delete results and grades for a student, which are then graded and analyzed. Students can access and print their result slips, and teachers aand admins can track the school's academic progress using the integrated analytical tools.
